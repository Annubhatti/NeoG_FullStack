Welcome to the World of BTS

BTS, also known as Bangtan Sonyeondan or "Bulletproof Boy Scouts," is a South Korean boy band that debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment (now HYBE Corporation). The group consists of seven members: RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook. Known for their dynamic

performances, deep lyrics, and wide-ranging musical styles, BTS has achieved global superstardom, building an incredibly dedicated fanbase known as "ARMY."
