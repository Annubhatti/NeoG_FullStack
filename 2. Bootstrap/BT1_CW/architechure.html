Architecture

The architecture of the Taj Mahal is a remarkable synthesis of Persian, Islamic, and Indian styles, showcasing the pinnacle of Mughal architectural excellence. Its design emphasizes symmetry, balance, and meticulous craftsmanship, making it one of the most admired monuments globally.

Made of white Makrana marble, known for its purity and ability to reflect light.
