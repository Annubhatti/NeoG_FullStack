History of Mumbai City

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, has a rich history that dates back centuries. Here are key milestones that shaped the city:

Ancient Period

Originally a group of seven islands, inhabited by fishing communities like the Kolis.

Colonial Era

Named "Bombaim," the Portuguese built churches and settlements after conquering the islands in 1534.

Buddhist Influence

Sites like Kanheri and Elephanta Caves show early Buddhist presence from 2nd century BCE.

Freedom Movement

Mumbai was a key center for India's independence movement, hosting major leaders and events.
