Early Life

Bill Gates, born William Henry Gates III on October 28, 1955, in Seattle, Washington, had a supportive and stimulating upbringing. His father, William H. Gates Sr., was a prominent lawyer, and his mother, Mary Maxwell Gates, was a businesswoman and civic leader. Gates grew up with two sisters, Kristi and Libby.

Co-founding Microsoft

Microsoft’s founding marked the start of Gates’ journey to becoming one of the world’s wealthiest and most influential individuals, reshaping the technology industry.

Software Innovations

At a time when most companies focused on hardware, Gates and Allen concentrated on creating and licensing software for personal computers. They believed software would be the driving force behind computing's future.

Philanthropy

Bill Gates is renowned not only for co-founding Microsoft but also for his extensive philanthropic work. Over the years, he has dedicated much of his time and resources to addressing global challenges, particularly through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, which he co-founded with his then-wife, Melinda Gates, in 2000. The foundation is one of the largest private charitable organizations in the world.

Global Impact

Gates was deeply involved in the technical and business sides of Microsoft, setting its vision and strategy.Microsoft grew rapidly, becoming a leader in software development. In 1985, the company launched Windows, a graphical operating system that became the standard for personal computers worldwide.
