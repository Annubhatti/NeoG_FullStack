Biography

William Henry Gates III, commonly known as Bill Gates, is an American business magnate, software developer, and Philanthropist. Born on October 28, 1955, in Seattle, Washington, Gates co-founded Microsoft and played a crucial role int the personal revolution.

Philanthropy

Bill Gates has been a leading philanthropist, particularly through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, which he co-founded with his then-wife, Melinda French Gates, in 2000. With a mission to tackle some of the world's most pressing issues, the foundation has focused on areas like global health, poverty alleviation, and education.
